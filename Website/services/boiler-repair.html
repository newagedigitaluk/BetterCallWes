COMMON PROBLEMS

Boiler Problems I Fix Every Day

No Hot Water

Whether it's a diverter valve, thermostat, or heat exchanger issue – I'll diagnose and fix it fast so you're not left without hot water.

No Heating

Radiators cold? Could be the pump, motorised valves, or a pressure issue. I carry common parts and can often fix it same day.

Boiler Leaking

Leaks from seals, valves, or the heat exchanger need urgent attention. I'll find the source and repair or replace the faulty component.

Low Pressure

Constantly topping up your boiler pressure? There's likely a leak in the system. I'll find it and fix it properly.

Error Codes

Seeing an error code on your boiler display? I'm familiar with all major brands' fault codes and can diagnose the issue quickly.

Strange Noises

Banging, whistling, or gurgling? These noises often indicate problems with the pump, heat exchanger, or air in the system.

Should You Repair or Replace?

I'll always give you honest advice. Sometimes a repair makes sense; sometimes a new boiler is the better investment. Here's my general rule of thumb:

Consider Repair If:

  • Boiler is under 10 years old
  • Repair cost is under £500
  • It's the first major fault
  • Parts are readily available

Consider Replacement If:

  • Boiler is 15+ years old
  • You've had multiple breakdowns
  • Parts are obsolete/expensive
  • Energy bills are very high
