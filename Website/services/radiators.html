Why Your Radiator Might Not Be Working

Radiators can stop working properly for several reasons. Here's what I typically find and how I fix it:

Cold at the top: Usually trapped air. A simple bleed often fixes it.
Cold at the bottom: Sludge buildup. May need flushing or a power flush.
Completely cold: Could be a stuck valve, air lock, or system issue.
All radiators lukewarm: Often low pressure or a boiler issue.
