Why Install an Outdoor Tap?

An outdoor tap makes garden tasks so much easier. No more trailing hoses through the house or filling watering cans at the kitchen sink. It's one of those home improvements that you wonder how you ever managed without.

  • Water the garden easily with a hose
  • Wash the car without running inside
  • Fill paddling pools and water features
  • Clean garden furniture and tools
  • Connect a pressure washer for cleaning

How Installation Works

Most outdoor taps are installed by connecting to an existing cold water pipe inside the house (usually in the kitchen or a downstairs utility room). I drill through the wall, run the pipework, and mount the tap outside.

1

Find supply

I locate the nearest suitable cold water pipe, usually under the kitchen sink.

2

Fit isolator

An inside isolator valve lets you shut off the outdoor tap for winter.

3

Drill through

A neat hole through the wall, with the tap positioned at a convenient height.

4

Mount tap

The tap is secured to the wall with a backing plate. Test and tidy up.
