What is Power Flushing?

Power flushing is a deep cleaning process for your central heating system. Over time, rust, sludge, and limescale build up inside your radiators and pipework. This debris restricts water flow, making your boiler work harder and your heating less effective.

Using a specialist pump, I flush clean water (with cleaning chemicals) through your system at high velocity. This dislodges and removes the sludge, restoring your heating to full efficiency.

Signs Your System Needs a Power Flush:

Radiators cold at the bottom
Radiators take ages to heat up
Brown or dirty water when bleeding
Pump is noisy
Boiler makes banging noises
Some radiators need constant bleeding

THE PROCESS

How Power Flushing Works

1

Initial Assessment

I'll check your system and confirm power flushing is the right solution. Sometimes a simpler radiator flush is enough.

2

Connect Equipment

The power flushing machine connects to your heating system, usually at a radiator or the boiler.

3

Flush Each Radiator

I isolate and flush each radiator individually, ensuring all sludge is removed. You'll see the dirty water clearing.

4

Add Inhibitor

Once clean, I add chemical inhibitor to protect your system from future corrosion and sludge buildup.
