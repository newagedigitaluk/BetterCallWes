SHOWER TYPES

Which Shower Type Do You Have?

Electric Showers

Heat water on demand from the cold supply. Independent of your boiler – great for backup.

Brands: Mira, Triton, Aqualisa, Gainsborough

Mixer Showers

Blend hot and cold water from your existing supply. Relies on your boiler/hot water system.

Better flow than electric, needs good water pressure

Power Showers

Mixer shower with built-in pump. Ideal for homes with gravity-fed hot water and low pressure.

Not suitable for combi boilers

Shower Questions

It depends on your electrical supply. Going from 8.5kW to 9.5kW is usually fine. Over 10.5kW may need an electrician to upgrade the cable and MCB. I'll check first.

For electric showers, often limescale buildup or a failing heating element. For mixer showers, could be a thermostatic cartridge issue or the hot water supply.
