BOILER TYPES

Which Type of Boiler Do You Need?

Combi Boilers

The most popular choice in the UK. Heats water on demand – no hot water tank needed.

Best for:

  • 1-3 bedroom homes
  • Properties with limited space
  • Households with 1-2 bathrooms
Typical Cost: £2,200 - £3,500 fitted

System Boilers

Stores hot water in a cylinder, allowing multiple taps to run simultaneously.

Best for:

  • 3+ bedroom homes
  • Multiple bathrooms
  • High hot water demand
Typical Cost: £3,000 - £4,500 fitted

Regular (Conventional) Boilers

Traditional setup with both a hot water cylinder and cold water tank in the loft.

Best for:

  • Older properties
  • Homes with existing tank setup
  • Very high hot water demand
Typical Cost: £2,800 - £4,000 fitted
