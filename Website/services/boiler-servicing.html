Why Annual Boiler Servicing Matters

Your boiler works hard – especially during the colder months. An annual service ensures it's running safely, efficiently, and keeps your manufacturer's warranty valid. Most boiler warranties require annual servicing by a Gas Safe engineer.

Landlord Gas Safety Certificates

By law, landlords must have an annual gas safety check (CP12) carried out by a Gas Safe registered engineer. This is separate from – but often done alongside – a boiler service.

I provide landlord gas safety certificates for £75, or combine it with a boiler service for a bundled rate. Certificates are issued the same day and can be emailed directly to you and your tenant.
