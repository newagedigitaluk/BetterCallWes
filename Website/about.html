About Us

Southampton's trusted plumber, dedicated to honest work and quality service.

OUR STORY

From Apprentice to Expert

I started my plumbing journey over 15 years ago as an apprentice, learning the trade from the ground up. After years of working for large companies, I founded Better Call Wes to provide Southampton with something different: a plumber who actually picks up the phone, shows up on time, and treats your home like their own.

Today, I've completed over 500 installations and helped thousands of families stay warm and comfortable. Whether it's a simple tap repair or a complete heating system overhaul, I bring the same attention to detail to every job.
