# Better Call Wes - Design System

## 1. Design Philosophy
**"Trusted Trade Professional"**
A clean, high-contrast, and action-oriented design. It focuses on large, readable typography, clear value propositions, and direct calls to action. The aesthetic is "New Age" trade - professional, established, yet modern and accessible.

## 2. Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Navy Deep**: `#0F2942` (Header, Footer, Headings) - A deep, trustworthy navy.
- **Safety Orange**: `#FF8C00` (Buttons, Highlights) - Vibrant orange for high-visibility CTAs.
- **Success Green**: `#25D366` (Trust Badges, checkmarks) - Used specifically for positive reinforcement.

### Secondary Colors
- **Hero Overlay**: `linear-gradient(90deg, rgba(15, 41, 66, 0.9) 0%, rgba(15, 41, 66, 0.7) 50%, rgba(15, 41, 66, 0) 100%)`
- **Background Light**: `#F8FAFC`
- **Text Grey**: `#64748B`

## 3. Typography
**Font Family**: `Inter` or `Roboto`, sans-serif.

### Scale
- **H1 (Hero)**: `3.5rem` (56px) - ExtraBold (800).
- **H2 (Section)**: `2.25rem` (36px) - bold (700), often centered.
- **H3 (Card)**: `1.25rem` (20px) - Bold (700).
- **Body**: `1rem` (16px) - Regular (400).

## 4. Components

### Navigation
- **Sticky Header**: Deep Navy background (`#0F2942`).
- **Links**: White text, refined spacing.
- **CTA**: "Book Online" and "07700 155 655" Button in Orange.

### Hero Section
- **Layout**: Full-width background image of a plumber/van.
- **Overlay**: Dark gradient fade from left to right.
- **Content**: Left-aligned text.
- **Headline**: Multi-line, highlighted keywords in Orange.
- **Actions**: Two large buttons side-by-side (Green "WhatsApp", Orange "Book Online").

### Features Grid (Why Choose Us)
- **Style**: Horizontal row of 4 items.
- **Icon**: Colored square icon (Green/Orange/Blue/Purple) with specific vector icon inside.
- **Text**: Centered label below icon.

### Service Cards
- **Structure**: Grid layout.
- **Card Style**: Image-heavy. Full background image.
- **Overlay**: Gradient overlay at the bottom of the card (`rgba(0,0,0,0.6)`).
- **Text**: White text positioned at bottom left of card.
- **Button**: "View Details" button embedded in the card or link.
- **Hover**: Image zoom or overlay lighten.

### Meet Wes
- **Layout**: Split screen.
- **Left**: Large portrait photo of Wes.
- **Right**: "Meet Wes" heading, bio text, and two stats cards (e.g., "10+ Years", "Gas Safe").
- **CTA**: "Read My Story" button.

### Testimonials
- **Style**: Cards with "Real Customer Stories" header.
- **Content**: Star rating, quote text, name, location.
- **Visual**: Simple white card with shadow.

### Service Areas
- **Style**: Row of badges/chips indicating postcodes (e.g., SO14, SO15).
- **Interactive**: Hover effects on badges.

### Footer
- **Style**: Dark Navy background with map/van image at bottom.
- **Content**: Address, services list, contact info.

## 5. Spacing
- **Container**: Max-width `1200px`.
- **Vertical Spacing**: `5rem` between sections.

## 6. Iconography
- **Set**: `Lucide Icons` or `FontAwesome`.
- **Style**: Filled shapes for "Why Choose Us" section.
